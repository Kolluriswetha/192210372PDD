Privacy Policy — Magic Words

Effective date: October 14, 2025. This Privacy Policy explains how Magic Words collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Introduction

Magic Words ("we", "our", or "the App") is an AI-powered story-creation application for Android devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information transparently. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, username, email address, profile image (if provided), age group, and preferences when you register or edit your profile.
  • Authentication data: login credentials (securely stored and typically hashed on the server), OAuth tokens if you use Google Sign-In.
  • Content you create: story prompts, generated stories, titles, edits, and any files you choose to save or export (.txt, .pdf, .docx).
  • Usage data: app activity such as features used, screens visited, language settings, and story generation history.
  • Technical data: device model, operating system version, IP address, crash logs, and app version for diagnostic purposes.
  • Feedback: ratings, emoji reactions, and messages you submit through the feedback form.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features (including story generation).
  • Process account registration and authentication, and to communicate with you about your account.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., remembering favorite genres, font settings, and story history).
  • Analyze usage and app performance, diagnose technical issues, and fix bugs.
  • Respond to feedback and support requests.
  • Detect, prevent, and address security and abuse issues.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: third-party vendors who perform services for us (hosting, analytics, crash reporting, email delivery). These providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
  • Business transfers: if the App is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction (we will notify you where required by law).
  • With your consent: when you ask us to share or publish content (for example, if you choose to share a story publicly).

5. Third-Party Services & Links

Magic Words may use third-party services such as analytics providers, crash-reporting tools, and cloud hosting. These services collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those third parties. The App may contain links to third-party websites; we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

6. Analytics and Cookies

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with Magic Words. Analytics help us improve the App’s performance and user experience. We do not use cookies in the traditional browser sense for the native Android app, but we may store small data on the device (local storage) to remember settings and preferences.

7. How We Store and Secure Your Data

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. This includes encryption in transit (HTTPS) and other access controls. However, no system is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security of data. If a data breach occurs that affects your information, we will comply with applicable laws and notify you as required.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. You may delete your account to remove your profile and associated data — see section 10 for your rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Magic Words is not intended for children under 13 (or higher age if required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and local laws, you may have rights including:

  • Accessing the personal information we hold about you.
  • Requesting correction or deletion of your data.
  • Objecting to or restricting certain processing activities.
  • Receiving a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights or to request account deletion, contact us using the information below. We will respond to valid requests in accordance with applicable law.

11. International Data Transfers

We may transfer information to countries outside your own for processing and storage (for example, to cloud providers). Where required, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide a prominent notice in the App or by email. The "Effective date" at the top shows when this policy was last updated.
